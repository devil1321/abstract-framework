Transitions

Props:

  • data-transition_in - Applied to target div or element with listener.
  • data-transition_out - Applied to target div or element with listener.
  • Available Transitions In With fade class:
    • fadeIn
    • fadeInWithMove
    • fadeInWithRotateX
    • fadeInWithRotateY
  • Available Transitions In With slide-y class:
    • fadeInWithSlideY
    • slideInYTop
    • slideInYCenter
    • slideInYBottom
  • Available Transitions In With slide-x class:
    • fadeInWithSlideX
    • slideInYWithMove
    • slideInXLeft
    • slideInXCenter
    • slideInXRight
    • slideInXWithMove
  • Available Transitions In With scale class:
    • ScaleIn
  • Available Transitions Out With fade class:
    • fadeOut
    • fadeOutWithMove
    • fadeOutWithRotateX
    • fadeOutWithRotateY
  • Available Transitions Out With slide-y class:
    • fadeOutWithSlideY
    • slideOutYTop
    • slideOutYCenter
    • slideOutYBottom
  • Available Transitions Out With slide-x class:
    • fadeOutWithSlideX
    • slideOutYWithMove
    • slideOutXLeft
    • slideOutXCenter
    • slideOutXRight
    • slideOutXWithMove
  • Available Transitions Out With scale class:
    • ScaleOut
